What does it do?

Fake News Detector helps you distinguish between a real news and a fake news. Fake news stories on the internet and social media has been at the center of detailed speculations for years now. Our unique fake news detection algorithm takes a news input and analyses it to ascertain whether it is real or fake.
Another interesting fact about this application is that you can select between different algorithms to make your predictions.
